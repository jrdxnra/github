About Me

At Real Raw Fitness, I believe that fitness is more than just a physical activity. It's a way of life. I am a NASM Certified Personal Trainer with two years of experience, and I know firsthand the importance of discipline and fitness in achieving one's goals. My journey towards fitness started in my early years through sports and was further enhanced during my military career. I'm passionate about helping you achieve your fitness goals and live your best life.
